LIFTREPS

The no-nonsense gym workout tracker for iPhone & Apple Watch.

Coming soon to the App Store

What it does

LIFTREPS is a weight-lifting log built for people who actually lift. Log a set in two taps, follow your weekly split, and let the rest timer run on your lock screen and Apple Watch. No account, no ads, no analytics — your data lives on your device and, if you keep backup on, in your own private iCloud. Never on our servers.

Features

  • Fast set logging — weight/rep steppers pre-filled from your last session
  • Rest timer — full-screen countdown, lock-screen Live Activity, and local notification
  • Apple Watch app — log sets, see plate breakdown, and skip rest from your wrist
  • Progress charts — weight, reps, and estimated performance per exercise
  • Plate calculator — plates-per-side for your barbell and available plates
  • 870+ exercise library — photos, instructions, and muscle groups
  • AI plan import — paste a JSON split from ChatGPT or Claude and start training
  • RPE & notes — per-set effort tracking, body weight log, kg/lb

Privacy

Everything is stored on-device in SQLite, with an optional backup to your own private iCloud. No account, no analytics, no tracking. Read the full privacy policy.
