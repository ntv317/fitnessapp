Privacy Policy

App: TRAK  |  Effective date: June 4, 2026

Short version: TRAK stores your workout data only on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

1. Information We Collect

TRAK does not collect any personal information. All data you enter — workout plans, exercise logs, weight and rep counts — is stored locally on your iPhone using on-device SQLite storage and never leaves your device.

2. Data Storage

All workout data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. Data is not backed up to any external server operated by us. If you use iCloud Backup, Apple may back up app data as part of your device backup under Apple's own Privacy Policy.

3. Apple Watch Sync

If you use the TRAK Apple Watch companion app, workout state (current exercise, set number, rest timer) is transmitted between your iPhone and Apple Watch via Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This data travels only between your own devices and is not accessible to us.

4. Notifications

TRAK may ask permission to send local notifications to alert you when your rest period ends. These notifications are scheduled on-device and no notification content is sent to external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

TRAK does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

6. AI-Generated Workout Plans

The AI Import feature allows you to paste JSON workout plans you have generated using a third-party AI service (such as ChatGPT or Claude). TRAK only reads and parses the text you paste — it does not communicate with any AI service directly, and no data is sent anywhere by the app during this process.

7. Children's Privacy

TRAK does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no features designed for children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review it periodically.
