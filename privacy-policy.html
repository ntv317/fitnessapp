Privacy Policy

App: LIFTREPS  |  Effective date: July 5, 2026

Short version: LIFTREPS stores your workout data only on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

1. Information We Collect

LIFTREPS does not collect any personal information. All data you enter — workout plans, exercise logs, weight and rep counts — is stored locally on your iPhone using on-device SQLite storage. We operate no servers of our own and never receive, transmit, or store your data.

2. Data Storage & iCloud Backup

All workout data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. LIFTREPS can also back this data up to your own private iCloud — the app's iCloud Drive container — so you can restore it on a new device. This backup is enabled by default and can be turned off any time in the app's Backup settings. It lives entirely within your personal iCloud account, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy; it is never sent to any server operated by us, and we cannot access it. Separately, if you use iOS iCloud Backup at the system level, Apple may also include app data in your device backup.

3. Apple Watch Sync

If you use the LIFTREPS Apple Watch companion app, workout state (current exercise, set number, rest timer) is transmitted between your iPhone and Apple Watch via Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This data travels only between your own devices and is not accessible to us.

4. Notifications

LIFTREPS may ask permission to send local notifications to alert you when your rest period ends. These notifications are scheduled on-device and no notification content is sent to external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

LIFTREPS does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

Exercise demonstration images are loaded over the internet from a public content delivery network (jsDelivr, serving the open-source free-exercise-db dataset). These requests contain no personal data or identifiers beyond what any standard web request includes (such as your IP address, which we never see or store).

6. AI-Generated Workout Plans

The AI Import feature allows you to paste JSON workout plans you have generated using a third-party AI service (such as ChatGPT or Claude). LIFTREPS only reads and parses the text you paste — it does not communicate with any AI service directly, and no data is sent anywhere by the app during this process.

7. Children's Privacy

LIFTREPS does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no features designed for children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review it periodically.
